We collect information you provide when placing orders, including:

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate about your order status
  • Ensure food safety and allergen requirements
  • Improve our products and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Delivery services when applicable

Data Security

Your data is protected: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
