Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09 October 2025

Introduction

Cupcakes on the Avenue (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and place orders.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Delivery Address
  • Toppings or custom order requests
  • Delivery preferences (date, method)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your order
  • Communicate with you about your order status
  • Send you order confirmations or receipts
  • Improve our website and services

Payment Information

Payments are processed through secure third-party services (e.g., SumUp). We do not store your payment card details on our website. All payment processing follows strict security standards.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties except for trusted service providers who assist us in processing payments or delivering your orders, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device to help improve site navigation and functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at CupcakesontheAvenue@gmail.com.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
