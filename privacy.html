Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

What We Collect

When you place an order or make an enquiry we may collect your name, contact details and order preferences. Payment is processed securely via Stripe — we never store card details.

How We Use It

Your details are used solely to process and fulfil your order. We will never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Push Notifications

If you subscribe to notifications on our site, we may send updates about new bakes and market days. You can unsubscribe at any time using the bell icon on our website.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. By continuing to use the site you consent to their use. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact us at cupcakesontheavenue@gmail.com.
