🚚 Delivery & Collection Policy

Minimum order lead time is 7 days. For urgent orders, call 07842817789 and we'll try to help!

📍 Delivery Areas

We deliver to:

  • Swindon town center and surrounding areas
  • Highworth and nearby villages
  • Within 10 miles of our kitchen
  • Special arrangements for larger orders outside this area

💰 Delivery Charges

  • Market Collection: FREE
  • Home Collection: FREE
  • Local Delivery (within 5 miles): £3.50
  • Extended Delivery (5-10 miles): £5.00
  • Orders over £50: FREE local delivery

📅 Delivery Times

Delivery Windows:

  • Morning: 9am - 12pm
  • Afternoon: 1pm - 5pm
  • Evening: 6pm - 8pm (weekends only)

We'll contact you 30 minutes before delivery to confirm you're available.

📞 Contact for Delivery

To arrange delivery or collection:

  1. Call us at 07842817789
  2. Provide your full address and postcode
  3. Choose your preferred delivery window
  4. We'll confirm availability and delivery fee

💳 Payment Terms

Payment Schedule:

  • 25% deposit required when placing your order
  • Remaining 75% must be paid 24 hours before collection/delivery
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer, card payment, or cash
  • Orders will not be prepared without deposit payment

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Please be available during your chosen delivery window
  • Someone over 18 must be present to receive the order
  • We'll call if running late or early
  • Final payment must be completed 24 hours before collection/delivery
  • Special dietary requirements must be mentioned when ordering
