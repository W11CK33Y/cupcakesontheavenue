Delivery & Collection Policy

Lead time

Minimum order lead time is 7 days. For urgent orders, call 07842817789 and we’ll try to help.

Collection

  • Location: Highworth Market (details provided in your confirmation).
  • Collection window will be agreed in advance; please arrive on time to keep your bakes fresh.

Local delivery

  • Delivery area and fee are agreed at checkout or via message.
  • We’ll provide a delivery window; if no one is available, we’ll attempt to call. Re‑delivery may incur a fee.

Allergens

We handle the 14 major allergens. While care is taken, cross‑contamination risk cannot be entirely eliminated. Please inform us of any allergies when ordering.

Weather and traffic

Severe weather or traffic may affect delivery windows. We’ll keep you updated if delays occur.
