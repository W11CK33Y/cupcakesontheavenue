Refunds, Returns & Cancellations

Most of our products are made-to-order and perishable. The below outlines how refunds and cancellations work.

Change of mind

As our items are perishable and often custom-made, we cannot accept returns or provide refunds for change of mind once production has begun.

Quality issues or damage

  • Please contact us within 24 hours of collection/delivery with your order reference and clear photos.
  • We will investigate and may offer a repair, replacement, partial refund, or full refund depending on the issue.

Cancellations

  • 7+ days before your date: full refund.
  • Within 7 days: ingredients and labour are allocated; cancellations may be partially or non‑refundable.
  • Custom designs or special orders may require a non‑refundable deposit.

Order changes

We’ll try to accommodate changes up to 7 days before your date. Within 7 days, changes may not be possible or may incur a fee.

How to request

Email cupcakesontheavenue@gmail.com or call 07842 817 789 with your order reference.
