💰 Refunds & Cancellations

📞 Contact Information

For all refund and cancellation requests, please contact us at 07842817789 or email cupcakesontheavenue@gmail.com

⏰ Cancellation Policy

Notice Period: Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before your scheduled pickup/delivery time for a full refund.

💸 Refund Timeframes

  • 48+ hours notice: Full refund processed within 3-5 business days
  • 24-48 hours notice: 50% refund due to preparation costs
  • Less than 24 hours: No refund available (preparation may have begun)

🎂 Custom Orders

Special Conditions: Custom cakes and special occasion orders require 72 hours notice for cancellation due to specialized ingredients and preparation time.

🚫 Exceptions

Refunds will be provided regardless of timing in cases of:

  • Severe weather preventing pickup/delivery
  • Illness or emergency situations
  • Quality issues with our products
  • Our inability to fulfill the order

💳 Refund Method

Refunds will be processed back to your original payment method. Please allow 3-5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

📋 How to Request

To request a cancellation or refund:

  1. Call us at 07842817789
  2. Provide your order details and reason for cancellation
  3. We'll process your request and confirm the refund amount
  4. Receive confirmation via email or text
